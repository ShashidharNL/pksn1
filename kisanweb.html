The Farmer's Challenge

We started with a simple, powerful user story.

"As a small-scale farmer in rural India, I struggle with identifying crop diseases, understanding volatile market prices, and accessing complex government schemes, often due to language barriers, limited literacy, and lack of internet access. I need an easy-to-use, reliable, and localized source of information that helps me make informed decisions to improve my yield and income."

Our Solution: A Voice-First AI Assistant

Project Kisan is an accessible, voice-first AI assistant designed to provide real-time, localized information, enhancing agricultural productivity and economic well-being for small-scale Indian farmers.

Interactive Features

This section details the core functionalities of Project Kisan. Click on each tab to explore how we leverage Google's AI to build each feature, creating a comprehensive and intelligent tool for farmers.

AI-Powered Crop Disease Diagnosis

Farmers can describe symptoms verbally or upload an image of a diseased plant. The system uses multimodal AI to identify the disease and suggests effective, accessible remedies.

Google AI Implementation:
  • Gemini Pro (Multimodal): Processes both voice descriptions (via Speech-to-Text) and uploaded images simultaneously for a highly accurate diagnosis.
  • Retrieval Augmented Generation (RAG): Gemini references a curated knowledge base of remedies stored in Cloud Storage for relevant and safe suggestions.
  • Vertex AI Vision (Optional): A custom model can be trained on specific local disease datasets for even higher accuracy on niche problems.

Real-Time Market Trend Analysis

Provides daily market prices for specific crops, analyzes trends, and offers insights on optimal selling times to maximize farmer profits.

Google AI Implementation:
  • Cloud Functions: Trigger scripts to ingest daily commodity prices from sources like AGMARKNET.
  • BigQuery & Firestore: Store historical data for trend analysis (BigQuery) and provide quick access to daily prices (Firestore).
  • Gemini Pro: Synthesizes trend data into simple, actionable advice delivered to the farmer in their local language via Text-to-Speech.

Simplified Government Scheme Navigation

Helps farmers discover relevant agricultural schemes, explains complex eligibility criteria, and guides them through the application process in simple terms.

Google AI Implementation:
  • Vertex AI Embeddings: Create embeddings from government scheme documents stored in Cloud Storage to make them searchable.
  • RAG with Gemini Pro: Gemini uses the most relevant document snippets to answer farmer queries accurately and conversationally.
  • Firestore Personalization: Stores farmer profiles (e.g., land size, crops) to filter and suggest only the most relevant schemes.

Risks & Mitigations

Risk: AI model inaccuracy.
Mitigation: Rigorous prompt engineering, show confidence scores, and advise consulting local experts.
Risk: Poor rural internet connectivity.
Mitigation: Build as a Progressive Web App (PWA) with offline capabilities and optimize for low bandwidth.
Risk: Low user adoption/digital literacy.
Mitigation: Extreme focus on simple, voice-first design. Conduct user testing and partner with local NGOs for outreach.
